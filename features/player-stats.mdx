## Overview

Player profile pages provide in-depth statistics, career information, and visual analytics for MLB players past and present.

## Player Profile Header

Each player page features:

- **Player Name**: First name and last name prominently displayed
- **Headshot**: High-quality player photo (silo for active players, action shots for retired)
- **Jersey Number**: Current uniform number
- **Position**: Primary position abbreviation (P, C, 1B, 2B, SS, 3B, OF, DH)
- **Team Association**: Current team or most recent team
- **Action Controls**:
  - Add to comparison tool
  - Add to favorites

## Career Statistics

Player stats are organized into hitting and pitching categories with interactive toggles.

### Hitting Statistics

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Available Stats">

    - **AVG** - Batting average
    - **HR** - Home runs
    - **RBI** - Runs batted in
    - **H** - Hits
    - **2B** - Doubles
    - **3B** - Triples
    - **BB** - Walks
    - **SB** - Stolen bases
    - **SLG** - Slugging percentage
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Year-by-Year">

    - Compare performance trends over time

### Pitching Statistics

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Available Stats">

    - **ERA** - Earned run average
    - **WHIP** - Walks + hits per inning pitched
    - **IP** - Innings pitched
    - **K** - Strikeouts
    - **BB** - Walks

<Note>
For two-way players like Shohei Ohtani, both hitting and pitching stats are available and can be toggled between.
</Note>

## Hot and Cold Zones

### Strike Zone Heat Maps

For batters, interactive strike zone visualizations show:

- **Hot Zones**: Areas where the player hits well (highlighted in green/yellow)
- **Cold Zones**: Areas where the player struggles (shown in blue/gray)
- **Batting Average**: By zone location
- **Pitch Type Breakdown**: Performance against different pitch types

### Pitch Location Analysis

For pitchers, zone charts display:

- Where pitches are most effective
- Opponent batting average by zone
- Strike percentage by location
- Pitch type distribution

<Tip>
Hot/cold zones update throughout the season based on recent at-bats and provide valuable insights into player tendencies.
</Tip>

### Stat Selection

The stat selector features:

- Drag-and-drop reordering (on supported devices)
- Active stat highlighted with accent color
- Abbreviations explained on hover
- Automatic switching between hit/pitch stats

## Structured Data

Player pages include rich schema.org markup for:

- Search engine optimization
- Rich snippets in search results
- Social media previews
- Voice assistant compatibility

This ensures player information appears correctly across:
- Google Search
- Social media shares (Twitter, Facebook, etc.)
- Smart assistants (Siri, Google Assistant)

<Warning>
Some stats may not be available for players from earlier eras due to limited historical data collection.
</Warning>
