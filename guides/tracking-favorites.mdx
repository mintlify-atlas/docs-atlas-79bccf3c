## Overview

The favorites system lets you save teams and players for quick access. Your favorites are stored locally in your browser and persist across sessions.

## Adding Favorites

You can favorite both teams and players throughout the app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the Star Icon">
    Look for the star icon next to team names and player profiles. An empty star means the item is not favorited.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click to Favorite">
    Click the star icon to add the team or player to your favorites. The star will fill with color to indicate it's been favorited.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm in Sidebar">
    Check the **Favorites** section in the sidebar to see your newly added favorite.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Where to Add Favorites

- **Team Pages** - Click the star icon in the team header
- **Player Pages** - Click the star icon next to the player name (located in the header after section)
- **Game Cards** - Star icon appears on team logos within game cards

## Viewing Your Favorites

All your favorites are accessible from the sidebar.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Favorites Section">
    Click the **Favorites** accordion in the left sidebar. It displays a star icon and shows "Favorites" text on wider screens.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Saved Items">
    Your favorites are displayed in a grid layout:
    - **Teams** appear first with team logos
    - **Players** appear below teams with player headshots
    
    Each favorite shows the team/player name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click to Navigate">
    Click any favorite to navigate directly to that team or player page.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
The favorites section automatically opens when you have at least one favorite saved.
</Tip>

## Removing Favorites

Removing favorites is just as easy as adding them.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the Page">
    Go to the team or player page you want to unfavorite.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click the Filled Star">
    Click the filled star icon to remove the item from your favorites.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Verify Removal">
    The star will become empty, and the item will disappear from your favorites sidebar.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits of Using Favorites

### Prioritized Game Display

Games involving your favorite teams automatically appear at the top of each status section (Live, Preview, Final) on schedule pages.

### Quick Access

Access your most-watched teams and players directly from the sidebar without searching or navigating through menus.

### Personalized Experience

The app remembers your favorites across sessions, so you don't need to re-add them every time you visit.

## Managing Storage

Favorites are stored in your browser's local storage:
- They persist across browser sessions
- They're specific to each browser and device
- Clearing browser data will remove your favorites

<Warning>
Favorites are stored locally and won't sync across different browsers or devices. If you clear your browser data, you'll need to re-add your favorites.
</Warning>

## No Favorites Yet?

If you see "No favorites" in the sidebar:
1. Browse to team or player pages
2. Click the star icon to start building your favorites list
3. The sidebar will automatically update to show your selections
